Whether your pet needs daily pills for heartworm prevention or post-surgery antibiotics, consistency is key. Here are five tips that actually work.

## 1. Set Alarms at the Same Time Every Day

Pets thrive on routine. If you give medication at 8 AM and 8 PM, stick to it. Your body will start to expect it too.

Give the pill right after your morning walk or before your evening news. Habit stacking works.
